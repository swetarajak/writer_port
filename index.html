About Me!

Hello,

I am Insanewriter and I welcome you all !


We together here make a family and share what we feel from inside so that we can have a connection and the feelings of loneliness gets over. In this era where people are always busy, craving for more and more, running behind materialistic beauty and many more. Sometimes people lost their ways, way of being happy, way of being true to themselves and needs a direction rather needs someone to hold their hands and help them or just remind them the correct path. Here, the insanewriter came all this way to be at your solace to remind you all the ways, to connect with you and your soul, to hold you, to once again mould you and cast you into the real you!


The insanewriter hopes that you will be able to get all your queries solved, and will be the happiest to connect with you all! In case you are struck don't hesitate to comment down below the posts or write to me in my insta and facebook handle! It's a place where you can freely share your thoughts and other may too get inspirations from it!


So, let's connect soul to soul!
Your one smile makes my day!
